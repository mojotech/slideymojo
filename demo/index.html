  • Why slideyMojo?

    Because you realize the importance of clean, responsive, reusable code.

  • Why not other sliders?

    Simply put, there is not another slider out there with the same cleanliness and attention to the needs of today's responsive designs.

  • How do I get started?

    Just download and include both jQuery and jQuery UI, then get yourself a copy of slideyMojo and include that too.

  • Magic one liner

    $('#sliderElement').slideyMojo('2');
